Workshop 1
Igniting Insight started out as an organisation managed by a small set of individuals with high spirits and limited resources. While our resources were limited, our belief in the organisation and its vision was tremendous, and it is with this belief, Igniting Insight organised its first event, Workshop 1. The Workshop 1 was to include subjects like Science, Maths, English, Tales of Mind Learning and Indian Knowledge and Tradition. Workshop 1 was organised for students from 5th to 8th standard. Over 150+ students registered for Workshop-I The workshop did not adhere to the syllabus defined by any educational board, but rather focused on a certain set of topics and ensured that all concepts pertaining to them were made clear to each student. We held exams at the end of the Workshop to help students work on their weak subjects.
Duration:- 20 days from 15th June 2020 to 4th July 2020.
Mode:- Fun Interactive Online Session
Workshop 2
In the month of November, Igniting Insight organised its second workshop for students from 5th to 8th Standard. While Workshop -II was quite like Workshop-I in terms of the way it was organised, our new and bigger team ensured that we could take a greater number of classes for each subject as well as tackle more concepts in greater detail, laying the foundation for our students to build their ambitions on. Over 100+ students registered for Workshop-II The workshop did not adhere to the syllabus defined by any educational board, but rather focused on a certain set of topics and ensured that all concepts pertaining to them were made clear to each student.
Duration: - 10 days from 9th November 2020 to 13th November 2020 and from 17th November 2020 to 21st November 2020.
Mode:- Fun Interactive Online Session
